4.2.6 Business Contact Details

 

4.2.6.1 Business Contact Details Homepage

Description

This page is the home screen for the business contact details, on this page the user can add new business contact details, search, update and delete the business contact details.

 

Reference:

Control:

Functionality Description:

1

Business Contact Details link

Click here to navigate to the business contact details homepage.

2

Add Details Button

Click here add new business contact details. System will redirect to Add Details page.

3

Show number of entries list

Click here to display only a certain number of business contact details.

4

Search input box

Click here to enter and search business contact details

5

Update button

Click here to update the business contact details. System will redirect to update business contact details home page.

6

Delete button

Click here to delete business contact details

7

Username link

Click here to view profile or to sign out of the system.

8

Return Home

Click here to return to the home page

•         Possible Error Messages: No data available in table

 

4.2.6.2 Add Business Contact Details

Description

This page allows the user to be able to add new business contact details. The user enters the contact details in the relevant input boxes and the system saves the details in the database.

 

Reference:

Control:

Functionality Description:

1

Company name input box

Click here to enter the name of the company in the input box.

2

Company email address input box

Click here enter the company email address in the input box.

3

Company phone number input box

Click here to enter the company phone number in the input box.

4

Company website Input Box

Click here to enter the company website in the input box

5

Add button

Click here to add the details completed to the database. The system will redirect to the business contact details homepage

6

Return to list button

Click here to cancel operation and return to business contact details home page.

•         Possible Error Messages:

o   Company name input box → Company name is required

o   Company email address input box → Company email address is required

o   Company phone number input box → Company phone number is required

o   Company website input box → Company website is required
